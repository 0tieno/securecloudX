# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in securecloudX a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Sharing malicious code, exploits, or tools intended to cause harm
- Using securecloudX content for unauthorized security testing
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to **securecloudx.learn@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate.
